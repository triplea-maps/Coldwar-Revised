Iron Blitz 1939A



Left unchecked in the post WW2-erea, Communism has spread and the West must now pay the price.
It is only a matter of time before the ignition is triggered and global war erupts again.

This scenario comes from the Iron Blitz computer game but has been adapted to the Revised Map..
